The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The dog barked at the fox, but the fox ignored the dog and continued jumping. A quick fox is better than a lazy dog, right? The quick brown fox has a bushy tail, and the lazy dog has a fluffy tail. 
People love watching the quick fox and the lazy dog—they are the most famous pair in animal stories. The fox’s quickness and the dog’s laziness make them perfect for teaching kids about opposites. Did you see the quick fox jump over the dog again? The dog didn’t mind; it just yawned and watched the fox run into the woods. 
Quick, quick, quick—how many times can the fox jump? The lazy dog counted: one, two, three jumps from the quick fox. The fox and the dog became friends after their many encounters, proving that quick and lazy can get along. The end of the story? The quick fox and the lazy dog lived happily ever after, with the fox visiting the dog every sunny afternoon.
